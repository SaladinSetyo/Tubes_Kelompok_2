SOLUSI (3 LANGKAH):

┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ LANGKAH 1: Add Apache Tomcat Library                  │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. Right-click project "PaulQuiz" di NetBeans
2. Pilih "Properties"
3. Sidebar kiri: click "Libraries"
4. Tab atas: click "Compile"
5. Click tombol "Add Library..."
6. Pilih "Apache Tomcat" (atau "Tomcat 9.0" atau "Tomcat 10.1")
7. Click "Add Library"
8. Click "OK"

┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ LANGKAH 2: Add JAR Files                              │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Masih di Properties → Libraries → Compile:

1. Click tombol "Add JAR/Folder..."
2. Navigate ke: C:\CODING\PaulQuiz\lib\
3. HOLD CTRL, lalu click SEMUA file .jar:
   - jbcrypt-0.4.jar
   - jstl-1.2.jar
   - mysql-connector-j-8.2.0.jar
   - standard-1.1.2.jar
4. Click "Open"
5. Click "OK"

┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ LANGKAH 3: Clean and Build                            │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. Right-click project "PaulQuiz"
2. Click "Clean and Build" (atau tekan Shift+F11)
3. Tunggu sampai muncul "BUILD SUCCESSFUL"
